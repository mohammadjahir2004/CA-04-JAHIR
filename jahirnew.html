ICST Founder

Founder’s VISION

The vision behind ICST is to make higher education a true force for change—breaking down barriers and opening doors for all. ICST was founded to deliver job-oriented, quality education to those with limited financial means, ensuring that every student, regardless of background, can access opportunity and empowerment. Our founder’s dream is a university where inclusivity is not just a word, but a reality—where students from disadvantaged backgrounds are given the same chance to succeed and thrive.
